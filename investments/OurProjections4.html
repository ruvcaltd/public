Option 2: Investment in high risk but high growth areas

Investment Strategy Name: HY22

Min Investment: Â£2,000,000

The Positives

  • Continuous cash flow meaning moderate yearly dividends which can be re-invested.
  • High capital growth.
  • High probability of gross return of at least 10% yearly, more likely above 15% annually when projected over a 5-year period. Gross returns include rental yield and capital growth.
  • Higher footfall through immigration etc can be used for our benefit.
  • Direct impact of market movement. Means upward trend will be more profitable in these areas.
  • Moderate maintenance costs as lesser number of units to handle due to high value investments.
  • Projections for 2+ years can be made, however 3+ years are more realistic.
  • Quick exit possible

The Negitives

  • Licencing and compliance: UK has too many tenant laws and we need to be always up to date to avoid fines and penalties. Licensing breaches are taken very seriously by the council.
  • Higher volatility in case of market meltdown. In that case there will be a requirement to hold the investments until the market recovers.
