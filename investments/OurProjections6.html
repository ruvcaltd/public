Our Projections

Option 2: Investment in high risk but high growth areas
How we invest here?

  • Market research: These areas need local knowledge. We cannot rely on government or privately owned data. Manual research is required.
  • Better negotiation on cash purchases as movers in conveyancing chain benefit from quick sale.
  • Buy mostly uninhabitable rundown properties and get to the standard of living while saving costs. This means we can also take advantage of some tax breaks that are not available when buying inhabitable properties.
  • Comply with local laws, find council contacts, and research best ways to add value to the property. Property extensions in these areas typically add a lot of value.
