Startup Partnership

If you have an idea which has the potential to turn into a successful tech business, we will partner with you to deliver it.

Software development is expensive business. Incorrect decisions, bad estimates add to the the cost. When working on a tight budget you need a partner.

Why? We grow when you grow; we succeed when you succeed. When we will have stake in your business you can rest assured we will proactively give our 100%.

Discuss

We organize an initial discussion, if we click, we sign a non-disclosure. You reveal your idea and we move on to a full workshop with proper brainstorming.

Appraise

We collaborate and spend some time on the market appraisal. Saving time and cost on this if the false economy.

Agree

If all goes well, we agree on the terms of our partnership. We will take the driving seat from there onwards.

Deliver

We deliver the product in time. We are always there for support and enhancements when required (remember we have our share in your business).

Ready to talk?

Do not hesitate to give us a call or write us an email with a brief description of your project. Do not worry if the idea is not refined yet, we can work on it together.

Startup Partnership FAQs

Answers to most frequently asked questions about the startup partnership program is given below. Don't hesitate to shoot us an email using the form below if you have any other specific question.

  • Is my startup idea safe?

    We sign a non-disclosure agreement before we start any work. This is a legally binding contract through which the parties agree not to disclose information covered by the agreement. An NDA creates a confidential relationship between the parties, typically to protect any type of confidential and proprietary information or trade secrets.

  • How much money can I save?

    We first work out the estimated total cost of your project, then we work out an estimated value of your business after five years (in most cases). Then we compute the net present value by adjusting for cost and inflation. You only pay the difference in cost. In many cases, this range between 30% to 55% of the total cost.

  • Will you accept all ideas?

    No. We will decide that after a thorough market appraisal. We will also be gauging your level of commitment to the project.

  • Can you help raise funds?

    Yes, we do have a network of individuals who are always looking for good tech investments. Once we have a working prototype, we can demo it to few investors and choose the best offer. That said, do not expect to get investments on day 1.

  • What happens after the product is delivered?

    You will be in-charge! However, we will continue to provide any king of technical assistance that may be required. It's a partnership - we grow when you grow.
